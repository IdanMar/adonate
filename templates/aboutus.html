Who are we?

Use us for a better world

We are 4 passionate 16 year old Palestinian-Israeli teenagers. We were asked as a part of our experience in a program named MEET to create a website that would create a social impact. We really care about our world so we didn't know what issues to specifically tackle in this project. Lastly we decided on creating a website that would help donate to everything. When you watch our ads we gain money and all that money would be donated to organizations around the world.

ADonate for making a change in the world!
